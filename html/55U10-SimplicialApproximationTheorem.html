simplicial approximation theorem


Let f:|K|→|L| be continuous function, where |K| and |L| are polyhedra having triangulations K and L, respectively.

Then there is a barycentric subdivision K(s) of K and a continuous function g:|K|→|L| such that g is a simplicial map from K(s) to |L| and g is homotopic to f.

The theorem is due to J.W. Alexander.

References
