0.1 Paracompact space examples

Locally compact Hausdorff spaces that are also σ-compact (http://planetmath.org/AlternativeDefinitionOfSigmaFiniteMeasure) can be shown to be paracompact, and, therefore, also normal. Other particular cases of paracompact spaces are those of locally compact Hausdorff spaces, second countable Hausdorff locally compact spaces, and their related locally compact groupoids.
